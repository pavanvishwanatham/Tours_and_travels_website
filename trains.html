Enjoy The Luxorious Trains in India

Maharajas Express

Adept at rendering your luxury tour an incredible twist, Maharaja Express is an award-winning luxury train that has grabbed the title for six consecutive years at the World Travel Awards. Offering eight grand luxury tours in India that are replete with an unparalleled experience of witnessing the opulent heritage as well as the architectural grandeur; and relishing the hospitality the country is famed for, Maharaja Express is truly an unforgettable experience. It is indeed your pass to see India in possibly the most luxurious way.

The Golden Chariot

Dubbed a romance on a rail track, Golden Chariot is an expedition that will guarantee millions of memories. An initiative by Karnataka Tourism Board, the luxury train takes you on a journey to some fabled and offbeat destinations of Karnataka which proffer pure travel bliss. Awarded in 2013 as Asia’s leading luxury train, the Golden Chariot is elegantly designed and comprises plush interiors and well-appointed cabins that are named after the ruling dynasties of Karnataka. Promising a luxury tour experience beyond imagination, it also boasts Ayurveda spa centre, fine-dining restaurants, and top-notch lounges to cater to all your luxury travel needs.

Palace on Wheels

The original luxury train that has been an inspiration for lavish heritage travel in India, Palace on Wheels is undoubtedly a not-to-miss experience in the country. Once used as transport means for the Maharajas of Rajasthan and the Nizams of Hyderabad, this heritage luxury train is best to see the Royal Rajasthan on wheels. Promising a voyage full of exuberance, this regal wagon casts its spell through heart-warming hospitality, splendid transport facility, and meticulously curated itineraries. An absolute experience, Palace on Wheels is the way to explore the royal side of India.

Deccan Odyssey

Reflecting the life of the Maharajas of the 16th Century, Deccan Odyssey is indeed luxury on wheels that redefines train travelling in India. This luxury train renders the opportunity to relish an expedition to explore the breathtaking world heritage sites, forts and palaces in six great rail journeys in 21 luxuriously appointed coaches. Expect to be pampered with the state-of-the-art-facilities of the multi-cuisine restaurant, well-stocked bar, high-tech conference cart and a spa & massage cabin onboard. Deccan Odyssey is not just a train journey, it is a sojourn full of magic, something that you have been missing out on all your life.
